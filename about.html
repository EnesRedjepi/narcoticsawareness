About Narcotics

Understanding the substances, risks, and facts.

Common Types

Opioids: Morphine, heroin, codeine, oxycodone.
Stimulants: Cocaine, methamphetamine.
Depressants: Barbiturates, benzodiazepines.
Hallucinogens: LSD, psilocybin.

UNODC Drug Types
Effects on Body and Mind

Narcotics alter the central nervous system, impacting mood, perception, and behavior. They can cause euphoria, but also confusion, drowsiness, and dangerous health risks.

Why Youth Should Care

Young people are especially vulnerable to peer pressure and misinformation. Awareness helps protect futures and supports making healthy choices.
