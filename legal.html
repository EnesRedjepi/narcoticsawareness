Legal Consequences

Understanding the law and your rights.

What about Drug Law?

Possession, distribution, and manufacturing of narcotics are illegal in most countries and come with serious penalties.

Common Penalties
  • Fines and heavy monetary penalties
  • Imprisonment ranging from months to decades
  • Permanent criminal records affecting education and employment
  • Mandatory rehabilitation and community service
Prevention and Awareness

Governments and organizations worldwide run campaigns, educational programs, and support services to prevent narcotics abuse.
