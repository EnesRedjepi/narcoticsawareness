Health Effects of Narcotics

Short-term and long-term consequences for body and mind.

Short-Term Effects
  • Euphoria or intense pleasure
  • Impaired judgment and coordination
  • Drowsiness or hyperactivity
  • Nausea and vomiting
Long-Term Effects
  • Addiction and dependency
  • Brain damage and memory loss
  • Respiratory and heart diseases
  • Mental health disorders like anxiety and depression
Impact on Youth

Youth brains are still developing, making them especially vulnerable to the harmful effects of narcotics, which can disrupt learning and emotional health.
